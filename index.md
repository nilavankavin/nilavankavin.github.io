- **Bilingual Interface:** Enjoy a user-friendly UI in both Urdu and English.
- **Digital Menu:** Easily browse dishes and drinks.
- **Shopping Cart:** Add items to your cart for easy checkout.
- **WhatsApp Ordering:** Place orders directly through WhatsApp.
- **Responsive Design:** Works well on both computers and mobile devices.

## 📖 How to Use Zayka

1. **Opening the App:** Run Zayka by clicking its icon on your desktop or in your applications folder.
2. **Choose Language:** Select either Urdu or English for your preferred interface.
3. **Browse Menu:** Click through the digital menu to see all available items.
4. **Add to Cart:** If you wish to order something, click on the item and then select "Add to Cart."
5. **Checkout:** When you’re ready, click on the cart icon and proceed to checkout.
6. **Order Confirmation:** You can confirm your order through WhatsApp for easy communication with the restaurant.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting
